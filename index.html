Al Capone: From Nobody to Notorious Gangster

Introduction:

In the Roaring Twenties and during the Prohibition era, a man named Al Capone, a poor immigrant, rose to power and established a criminal empire in Chicago, also known as the Windy City. He became one of the most notorious gangsters in American history. Involved in bootlegging, violence, and organized crime, Capone gained both power and notoriety. This website explores his early life, rise to power, criminal empire, and inevitable downfall.
